Us (Being Crazy)


The debate started in September 2022 when we each found out the other despises our favorite foods. We began keeping a spreadsheet of opinions on coconut vs pretzels after taking votes from our friends, family, and strangers. Soon we were assigned this project for our 8th grade Web Design class and we had a brilliant idea. We will make a website for a food truck that delves into the many wonders of coconut and pretzels. We hope you enjoy all our food truck has to offer and place you vote on coconut vs pretzels today!
